Quantum information, sensing, and learning

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Southern California advised by Prof. Quntao Zhuang. My interests spans quantum magic, quantum learning/tomography, quantum machine learning/variational algorithms and quantum error correction.

Before USC, I completed an MSc in Physics at the University of Calgary with Prof. David Feder and Prof. Barry Sanders, and a BSc in Physics at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.
